Museum of Privacy: A Walk Through Time

Explore four connected rooms from 1890 to a speculative future. This virtual museum traces the shift from privacy as personal seclusion to privacy as a structural problem shaped by data aggregation, inference, and surveillance infrastructures.

Controls: click Enter Museum • move with W A S D • mouse to look • Shift to walk faster • Esc to release cursor.
