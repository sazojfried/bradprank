The Museum of Privacy

A 3D walk through the history of losing control.

This is a small first-person museum. Walk through four rooms showing how privacy shifts from being left alone, to control over records, to aggregation and profiling, and finally to a speculative future where privacy survives mostly as a feeling.

Controls: click to enter • move with W A S D • look with mouse • hold Shift to move faster • press Esc to free the cursor.

Walkable exhibit
Move through the rooms from left to right. Stand near a glowing plaque to read it.
Theme: privacy doesn’t simply disappear — it gets redefined downward with each technological shift.
