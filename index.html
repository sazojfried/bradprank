Meme Outbreak: Yale Edition

A tiny, slightly cursed simulation of how a meme spreads across Yale's 14 residential colleges. Each turn, you choose one upgrade to tweak your meme's traits, then watch it propagate.

Turn 1
Overall Infection: 0%
Upgrade Points: 1

Meme Traits & Upgrades

In Dawkins-speak, you're optimizing the meme's "fitness." In practice, you're making it more cursed.

Win by infecting at least 80% of the overall population before the attention span of campus moves on. Each upgrade is basically you becoming a slightly worse person in the group chat.
